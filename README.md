# 📖 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the major challenges faced by telecom companies. Predicting customers who are likely to leave helps businesses improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

This project uses **Machine Learning** to predict customer churn and **K-Means Clustering** to segment customers into different groups based on their behavior and service usage. It also includes **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to identify important patterns and factors affecting churn.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Predict customers who are likely to churn.
* Analyze customer behavior using EDA.
* Segment customers into meaningful groups.
* Generate business insights for customer retention.
* Support data-driven marketing and customer relationship strategies.

---

**Dataset:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer information such as:

* Tenure Months
* Monthly Charges

* Internet Service
* Contract Type
* Payment Method
* Customer Status
* Churn Label
